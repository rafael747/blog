Focaccia is a type of Italian flatbread known for its soft, airy interior and crispy crust, traditionally flavored with olive oil and herbs

<!--more-->

## Ingredients

  - 600g all-purpose flour
  - 1 spoon sugar
  - 1 spoon salk
  - 1 spoon dry yeast or 30g fresh yeast
  - 500ml water

## Topping

  - fresh mozzarella
  - cherry tomatoes
  - olive oil
  - rock salt
  - oregano

## Instructions

  - Combine the flour, sugar, salt and yeast
  - Add the water and knead until smooth
  - Let it rest for 20 minutes
  - Knead again until dough is smooth and a bit elastic (add more flour if needed)
  - Let it rest for 12 hours outside the fridge
  - Let it rest for 12 hours inside the fridge 
  - Put the dough in an oiled baking tray. Massage the dough with more olive oil
  - Add the fresh mozzarela, tomatoes, salt and oregano
  - Let it rest for 1 hour
  - Bake for about 50 minutes at 200ÂºC
